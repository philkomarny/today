# Your Writing Voice

This file tells the social posts agent how to write in YOUR voice. Edit this to match your style.

## Tone
- Direct and concise
- Lead with your strongest claim
- End with a question or challenge, not a summary

## What to avoid
- Corporate buzzwords and jargon
- Hedging language ("it's worth noting", "it's important to consider")
- Generic platitudes that could apply to anyone

## Platform preferences

### LinkedIn
- 150-300 words
- Provocative thesis up front
- Use concrete numbers when possible
- Challenge conventional wisdom in your industry

### X / Twitter
- Under 280 characters when possible
- Thread format (2-3 tweets) for bigger ideas
- Quotable and shareable
- Tag specific people when replying to their ideas
